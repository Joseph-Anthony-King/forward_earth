[h1]FORWARD EARTH[/h1]

Like most people playing this game I love science fiction and this mod is a galaxy generation mod that presets the game in ways I like to play.  [b]Forward Earth[/b] sets up a sort of human space where a few early space human factions start near each other.

A new government type is added: Planetary Union.  The planetary union is oligarchic in nature and is kind of like the neo-liberal order in space.

There a few changes to the human factions.  The [b]United Nations of Earth[/b] is now an oligarchic planetary union that starts with civics efficient bureaucracy and diplomatic corps.  The [nb]United Federation of Earth[/b] is a democratic anarcho-syndicalist union that of course starts on Earth with civics beacon of liberty and idealistic foundation.  The [b]Commonweatlh of Man[/b] is now the [b]Commonwealth of Aurora[/b] which is a citizen stratocracy that starts on [i]Eta Cassiopeiae[/i] within the vicinity of [i]Sol[/i].  Rather than being a dictatorship it is more of a citizen republic with civics of meritocracy and citizen service.  There is also an NPC Human faction which will generally appear, the [i]League of Human Colonies[/i].  Their origin is similar to the [i]Commonwealth of Aurora[/i] except they arrived at a desert world.  They are only recently unified into an oligarchic authority with ethics of authoritarianism and fanatic materialists and civics of cuttthroat politics and warrior culture.  Altogether, they form a kind of human space which makes for some interesting early game play.

This mod also adds two additional start sizes: gigantic with 1,500 stars and immense with 2,000. This mod also includes the star names included in my [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1155894775]This is Alpha Ceti V![/url] mod. This mod includes a little over 5,000 names. I used a number of franchises, the Greek alphabet, Egyptian, Sumerian and Hindi mythology and an online name generator to generate the list (sorry but I forgot to get the link).

This mod took quite a bit of time and I used my programming skills to script a lot of this out.  Yet, as good as I think these scripts are my art abilities are abysmal.
